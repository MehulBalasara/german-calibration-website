On-Site Calibration

Your instruments don't always need to leave your facility. Our NABL-accredited on-site team delivers traceable calibration at your facility.

On-site accreditation is rare — most labs only hold permanent facility accreditation. We hold both.

What we bring to your site

  • Air gauge units
  • Bench centres — parallelism / coaxiality (up to 300mm)
  • Height gauges — vernier / dial / electronic (up to 1000mm)
  • Straight edges
  • Surface plates — granite / cast iron (up to 2000×2000mm)
  • 2d Electronic height gauges (up to 600mm)
  • Profile projectors / Video measuring machines
  • Surface roughness testers
  • Pressure devices — pneumatic (0–25 bar), hydraulic (0–700 bar), vacuum

Full NABL traceability. Zero transport risk.

Our on-site team operates under the same accreditation scope as our permanent facility. Calibration is performed at your premises — no instrument handling in transit, no production disruption, full certificate traceability.
