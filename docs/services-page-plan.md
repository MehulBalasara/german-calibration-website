## Division 1: NABL Accredited Calibration

### 1A — Dimensional Calibration (Permanent Facility)

**Do NOT list all 91+ instruments.** Instead: highlight standout/rare ones, group the rest logically, and link to full scope PDF.

#### Standout Highlights (rare — most local labs don't have these)

| Instrument | Why It's Notable |
|---|---|
| External Micrometers up to **1000mm** | Most local labs max at 300–500mm |
| Calipers (Vernier/Dial/Electronic) up to **2000mm** | Exceptionally large range |
| Surface Plates (Granite/Cast Iron) up to **2000×2000mm** | Small labs typically have 600×600 at best |
| Profile Projectors / Video Measuring Machines | Optical measurement — 10X–100X magnification, angular 0°–360° — rarely available locally |
| Reference Spheres (CMC **±0.7µm**) | Extreme precision, used for CMM calibration |
| Spline Plug & Ring Gauges | Specialized gear industry tooling |
| Lead Gauges & Form Gauges | Gear/thread manufacturing specialists |
| Inspection Fixtures / PCD Gauges up to **600mm** | Specialized for component inspection |

#### Instrument Groups (for page cards/sections)

**Group 1 — Basic Measuring Instruments**
Calipers (vernier/dial/electronic, up to 2000mm), Micrometers (external up to 1000mm, internal, depth, thread pitch), Height gauges, Depth gauges, Bore dial gauges, Dial comparators, Lever dial gauges, Coating thickness gauges, Feeler gauges

**Group 2 — Gauges**
Plain plug gauges (up to 500mm), Plain ring gauges (up to 300mm), Plain snap gauges (up to 800mm), Thread ring gauges (up to 500mm), Taper plug/ring gauges, Parallel thread plug gauges, Air gauge units, Spline plug/ring gauges, Dial snap gauges

**Group 3 — Surface & Reference Standards**
Surface plates — granite/cast iron (up to 2000×2000mm), Straight edges, Engineer's squares, Granite squares, V-blocks, Square masters/cylinders, Measuring pins, Micrometer setting standards (up to 775mm), Depth masters, Bevel protectors, Thread measuring wires

**Group 4 — Form, Thread & Inspection**
Form gauges, Lead gauges (radius/distance/depth/pitch/angle), Inspection fixtures, Relation gauges, CD/PCD gauges (up to 600mm), Templates, Thread pitch micrometers

**Group 5 — Precision & Optical Instruments**
Profile projectors / Video measuring machines (10X–100X, 0–300mm linear, 0–360° angular), Surface roughness testers + masters, Electronic height gauges (up to 600mm), Caliper checkers (up to 670mm), Dial calibration testers, Reference spheres (CMC ±0.7µm)

**CTA on this section:** "View our complete NABL scope (18 pages, 91+ instrument types)" → links to scope PDF download + NABL public verification URL

### 1B — Pressure Calibration (Permanent Facility)

| Type | Range | CMC Uncertainty |
|---|---|---|
| Pneumatic | 0 – 25 bar | ±0.052 bar |
| Hydraulic | 0 – 700 bar | ±0.8 bar |
| Vacuum | −0.85 to 0 bar | ±0.011 bar |

**Standout:** 700 bar hydraulic is rare — most regional labs stop at 100–200 bar. Vacuum capability is also uncommon locally.

### 1D — On-Site Calibration (Site Facility — NABL Accredited)

Performed at the **customer's premises**. Key differentiator: this facility is **also NABL accredited** — most labs only hold permanent facility accreditation.

**What we bring to your site:**
- Air gauge units
- Bench centers (parallelism/coaxiality, up to 300mm)
- Height gauges (vernier/dial/electronic, up to 1000mm)
- Straight edges
- Surface plates (granite/cast iron, up to 2000×2000mm)
- Electronic height gauges (up to 600mm)
- Profile projectors / Video measuring machines
- Surface roughness testers
- Pressure devices — pneumatic (0–25 bar), hydraulic (0–700 bar), vacuum

**Key message for this section:** "Your instruments don't always need to leave your facility. Our NABL-accredited on-site team brings the lab to you."
