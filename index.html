Fully Accredited

NABL Accredited Laboratory

German Calibration holds an active NABL accreditation under ISO/IEC 17025:2017 — the international gold standard for calibration laboratory competence.

Every calibration report we issue carries the NABL mark, giving your quality team, customers and auditors the confidence that measurements are traceable to national standards. As an ILAC-MRA signatory, our certificates are recognised in over 100 countries.

Measurement traceability — results linked to national and international measurement standards.

Audit-ready reports — accepted by quality auditors, IATF 16949, ISO 9001 and regulatory bodies.

Uncertainty quantified — every result includes calibration measurement capability (CMC) values.
